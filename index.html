GAIA-N-ECOLOGISTS For Mother EARTH

We are experts in plant ecology and soil science

We provive solutions to stakeholders whether in government or the private sector on fact-finding or training in ecology, particularly vegetation and soil science. Our aim is to execute client directives on resolution of natural history challenges. The most important challenges currently are how, we as humans, will adapt to global and climate changes that threaten contemporary livelihoods of human settlements and associated natural resources provided by the biological and physical environment. Chief amongst these are water and soil resources that are the backbone of human civilization and essential for our survival through contemporary crises.
